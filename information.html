About 25M Military Group

25M is a formidable military group known for its relentless operations against hostile forces. Our primary adversary has been the Greek factions, particularly those from Skopje. We specialize in executing strategic strikes, expertly disrupting enemy defenses, and sowing chaos within their ranks. With a combination of precision, tactical ingenuity, and an unyielding commitment to our cause, 25M has consistently been a thorn in the side of our enemies, leaving them disoriented and vulnerable to our next move.

Our Strengths

  • Precision and Strategy: We focus on well-planned, decisive strikes that target the enemy's most vulnerable points.
  • Experience: Our members are battle-hardened veterans, skilled in both traditional and unconventional warfare.
  • Adaptability: Whether in the mountains or urban environments, we adapt quickly to the battlefield, ensuring victory no matter the terrain.
  • Unity: 25M operates as a single, cohesive unit with a shared vision and unwavering loyalty to the cause.

Our Objective

Our ultimate goal is to destabilize our enemies, especially those who stand in opposition to the freedom and security of our people. We remain ever vigilant, ready to strike at any moment, and our legacy is built on fearlessness and determination in the face of adversity.
